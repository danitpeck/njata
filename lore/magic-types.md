**Magic Types**

Elemental Magic (fire, water, air, earth, life, darkness)  
**Fire:** Can be used to do attacks, to enhance (buff offense) or to deteriorate (debuff   
defense)  
**Water:** Can be used to heal, to do attacks, and to trap (debuff movement?)  
**Air:** Can be used to communicate, to do attacks, to knock out/cause sleep  
**Earth:** Can be used to heal, to attack, and to enhance (buff defense)  
**Life:** Can be used to heal, to pacify (debuff offense), and to communicate  
**Darkness:** Can be used to conceal, to cause sleep, and to dispel magic

Illusion Magic (sound, vision, smell, touch)  
**Sound:** Can be used to confuse (lower accuracy), to attack, or to communicate  
**Vision:** Can be used to conceal, to blind, to distract (lower accuracy) or to communicate  
**Smell:** Can be used to confuse (lower accuracy), to lure, to put to sleep or to attack   
(poisonous)  
**Touch:** Can be used to attack, to defend
